About Luxora

Luxora is a student-born engineering initiative focused on electric vehicle innovation. We blend mechanical design, power electronics, embedded software, and AI-driven efficiency to create practical, sustainable mobility solutions.

About the Founder: Benjamin Ndawana, a Mechanical Engineering student at Noida International University, is on a mission to transform ideas into impact. Through his startup initiative Luxora, he’s building the next generation of electric vehicles — combining creativity, sustainability, and engineering excellence to power a smarter world.

Vision

To create a world where electric mobility is not just an option, but a standard — efficient, affordable, and inspiring. Luxora envisions a future powered by smart, sustainable vehicles that blend technology, creativity, and responsible design to make transportation cleaner and smarter for everyone.

Mission

To engineer and prototype next-generation electric vehicles that embody innovation, sustainability, and practicality. Luxora aims to empower students and young engineers to design energy-efficient systems, build modular EV platforms, and advance research that contributes to a cleaner and more connected world.
